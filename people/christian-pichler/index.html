      Public Private Interoperability

      by Philipp Liegl, Christian Huemer, Christian Pichler

      Topic The research studio Public Private Interoperability (PPI) develops methods and tools targeting the inter-organizational integration of enterprises and organizations of the public sector. Service-oriented architectures and their implementation by Web Services are the current state-of-the-art technologies for this kind of integration problems.
